The Google ABC Book

Earlier and earlier kids are learning things online. So why not start as soon as possible? This ABC book curated by Google brings to your children the top searches for each letter! Kids will love it!

1. Choose a theme from your favorite Google service!

2. Print!

3. Follow the tutorial and make your instant ABC book!


A) Cut your following the boundaries. Beware of the detail at the bottom-left corner.

B) Fold it as indicated in the image above.

C) Cut following the marks in between the letters.

D) Fold the pages following the direction you made on step B. When joining the upside-down pairs, simply fold the paper vertically.

E) When you get to the end, join the front cover with the back of the last page using some glue.
